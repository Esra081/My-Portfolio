Who am I?

Hey! I'm Esra, a passionate developer who's part coder, part rocket enthusiast. Between September 2024 and February 2025, I worked as a part-time software developer at a startup, diving deep into computer vision. I helped build systems that detect objects and monitor spaces basically making sure nothing sneaky goes unnoticed.

When I'm not coding, I'm simulating rocket flights on MATLAB Simulink a passion that helped my team win 2nd place in Teknofest 2024's High Altitude Rocket Competition. I also took part in IREC 2025 (International Rocket Engineering Competition), a prestigious event in the USA that pushes the limits of student rocketry.

Currently, I'm focused on leveling up my web development skills from crafting neat interfaces to bringing ideas to life online. I'm a curious learner and a team player who's always excited about new projects and collaborations.
